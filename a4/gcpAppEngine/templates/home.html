(But not act like one)

Are you looking to travel to Niagara Falls? Do you not want to stand out to the locals as a tourist?

Follow this easy guide to not make the locals hate you!

Looking to get away next summer? Always wanted to see the "wonderful" Niagara Falls? Well then get up and go do it! Everyone wants to see the beautiful Niagara Falls once in their lives, and to see it is awe-inspiring. However, to those who live in Niagara the tourists can be annoying. The next 3 pages give you tips, planning advice and things to avoid while visiting Niagara Falls.

Questions to Avoid


Often as a local you get asked questions by tourists all the time! Some questions are understandable, and we locals will love to help; but other questions are downright stupid. Stupid questions should be avoided at all costs as sometimes locals like to mess with the tourists...

Types of questions that will gladly be answered

  • Facts about Niagara Falls
  • Directions to hotels or attractions
  • Places to park and eat

Types of questions to avoid

  • Our favourite attractions (we live here we rarely do them)
  • Directions to things you can see (happens more than you think)

Questions I have been asked...

  • What time does the rainbow turn off?
  • Is this Canada?
  • Why is this so expensive? (We do not set the prices!)
