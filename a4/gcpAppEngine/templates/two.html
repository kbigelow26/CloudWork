Ways to save money


Everyone knows that when you go on vacation you are going to be spending a lot of money. However, it is worth it to make memories and have a good time. In order to save yourself some money on your trip here are some tips that will keep some money in your pocket.

  • 1. Leave your car at your hotel and get a WEGO bus pass. Parking costs an insane amount anywhere you go in Niagara Falls. While the bus may take a bit longer it saves you money!

  • 2. Some places have a tax called the tourist tax. This tax is used for things like fixing roads in the tourist area, the free fireworks, and other things that keep the area running. But if you are on a tight budget then you can politely ask the person at the register if they can remove the tax. (Note: not all places by the falls have this, so trust the person at the register if they say they do not charge it) For a CBC article on the subject click here.

  • 3. Everyone loves the idea of not cooking while on vacation, but some prices are just ridiculous. The best way to save some money is to bring a lunch with you and have a picnic! Planning to spend money on a fun, exciting dinner works well but save yourself that lunch money to use at home or get more souvenirs.

  • 4. There are free attractions to take part in so you aren't paying for every minute of your visit. Their are fireworks nightly you can enjoy, view the floral clock, walk down the parkway, visit Dufferin Islands, and more.

  • 5. One of the simplest things that tourists always forget is a water bottle. Having to pay $2, $3, or sometimes even more for a bottle of water is just a waste. Bring your own bottle and fill it at fountains as you go!
